About Me

Bian Pasiliao

(BEE-yen pa-SEEL-yao)

is a Unity Game Developer based in NYC. She is currently engaging in contract work ranging from enhancing early prototypes and debugging past projects for Playmatics, to creating AI opponents in a multiplayer game for Dusk Sharp.

Although she did not start from having a formal game development education, her background in Computer Science and Interaction Design helped her learn quickly. She gained experience through completing a game development internship, participating in numerous game jams, and undertaking personal projects.

In the past, Bian has volunteered for the EGD Collective, a non-profit organization dedicated to helping students who aim to join the games industry. She enjoys attending game dev events, being creative with even more personal projects, and taking care of her plants.

Skills

  • Other Technical: Git, Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, MongoDB
