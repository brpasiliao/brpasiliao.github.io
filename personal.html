Personal Projects

What Am I? is a first-person narrative guessing game about putting yourself in others' shoes. Observe surroundings and feelings to discover unique perspectives. All features excluding music and audio were developed by me.

Click through the days, months and years in search of polaroids to regain lost memories. Look for the happiest day in this short, hour-long narrative clicker game. Programmed by me; written and art created by Tomasz. Winner of the GameFest 2023 Excellence in Narrative Award.

Froots! is a 2D top-down exploration game where a strawberry explorer finds an artifact that uproots him and his berry kind, and forces him to uncover the secrets of his fruit world. Programmed, and art assets created by me; written and designed by Anthony.

Dig through files to uncover a family history and a lost recipe in this narrative puzzle game called Lost in Sancocho. I took the role of programmer in a team of 4.

A solarpunk-themed RPG created for the EGD Collective's Game Studio Program. I was one of the 4 programmers in a larger team of about 20 people. Built the dialogue, camera, and NPC systems for the game.

Goomble is a 2D platformer game made for the GMTK Game Jam 2022. Programmed and developed by me in a team of 6.
